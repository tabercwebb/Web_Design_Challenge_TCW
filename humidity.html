Humidity

Surprisingly, there appears to be little to no correlation between humidity and latitude as one approaches the equator (0° degrees latitude). The scatterplot above shows a considerable amount of heterogeneity, even at similar latitudes. Interestingly, there is a small grouping of cities that exhibited abnormally low humidity levels (0% humidity) in the Northern Hemisphere between 60° and 75° latitude.
